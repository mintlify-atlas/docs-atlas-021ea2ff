---
title: "Sales"
description: "Record and track product sales at each location, with automatic stock deduction and reporting integration."
---

The sales module lets admins record product transactions at each TANCAT location. Sales are logged in the `ventas` table, stock is automatically adjusted, and all transactions are available for reporting.

## How sales work

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the sales page">
    Navigate to **Ventas** in the admin sidebar (`frontend/pages/ventas.html`). The page loads current products available at your location.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select products">
    Choose the product(s) being sold. Each item shows the current stock level and unit price. Enter the quantity for each item.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set payment details">
    Enter the total amount received and the payment method. Add any notes relevant to the transaction.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Record the sale">
    Submit the form. The backend writes a record to `ventas`, deducts the sold quantity from `productos.stock` for the relevant location, and returns the updated inventory state.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sales data

The `ventas` table captures each sale:

| Column | Type | Description |
|---|---|---|
| `id_venta` | integer | Primary key |
| `id_producto` | integer | FK to `productos` |
| `id_sede` | integer | FK to `sedes` — the location where the sale occurred |
| `cantidad` | integer | Units sold |
| `precio_unitario` | numeric | Price per unit at time of sale |
| `total` | numeric | `cantidad × precio_unitario` |
| `fecha_venta` | timestamp | When the sale was recorded |
| `id_empleado` | integer | FK to `empleados` — who recorded the sale |

## Stock deduction

When a sale is recorded, `ventasController.js` deducts stock from the product record at the matching `id_sede`:

```sql
UPDATE productos
SET stock = stock - :cantidad
WHERE id_producto = :id_producto
  AND id_sede = :id_sede
```

<Warning>
  The system does not prevent sales below zero stock. If `stock` drops to zero or below, the product will show negative stock in the inventory view. Admins should monitor stock levels and record purchase orders to replenish.
</Warning>

## Backend

Sales are handled by `controllers/admin/ventasController.js`, which exposes endpoints for:

- Listing sales with date range and location filters
- Recording a new sale (with stock deduction)
- Retrieving a summary for the dashboard

## Relationship with inventory

Sales and inventory are tightly linked:

```
productos.stock
    │
    ├── +  compras (restocked via purchase orders)
    └── -  ventas  (reduced on each sale)
```

This means the inventory view always reflects real-time stock after accounting for all recorded sales and received purchase orders.

## Reporting integration

All sales records feed into the reports module. From `reportes.html` you can generate:

- Total sales by date range
- Sales by location
- Sales by product
- Revenue summaries

See [Reports](/features/reports) for the full list of available report types.

## Access control

Sales access is location-scoped. Staff at Jacinto Ríos can only record and view sales for that location. The `scopeMiddleware.js` enforces this using the `id_sede` from the employee's JWT.
